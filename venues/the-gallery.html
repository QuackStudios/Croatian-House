The Gallery is a bright, fresh space finished in a clean, contemporary palette, offering an elegant blank canvas for optimal personalisation. Seating up to 300 guests for a banquet and 500 for a cocktail reception, it offers the ideal balance of scale and intimacy for weddings, cocktail functions, conferences, and corporate events. The private bar opens onto an outdoor verandah, creating natural flow between inside and out, while the refined neutral finish adapts effortlessly to any styling.

Venue Details

Large glass doors connect the main room to a private covered rear patio, giving events a natural indoor-outdoor feel. The patio can be used for guest arrivals, mingling, alfresco dining, breakout space, outdoor ceremonies or a more relaxed extension of the main function area.

The Gallery includes a modern service bar and access to a fully equipped commercial kitchen. This supports a range of catering formats, beverage arrangements and event styles, from formal seated service to cocktail functions, casual celebrations and supplier-led setups.

The venue is designed to be easy for guests, suppliers and event staff to access. The space includes accessible amenities and lift access, with on-site parking available for large events to help simplify arrival, departure and supplier logistics.

Is The Gallery suitable for weddings?

Yes. The Gallery works well for wedding receptions, ceremonies, engagement parties and celebrations that need a polished private space with room for dining, dancing and guest flow.

Can the outdoor patio be used during the event?

Yes. The covered rear patio can extend the event area and is well suited to arrivals, mingling, outdoor dining, breakout space or a relaxed secondary area for guests.

Does the venue support larger private functions?

Yes. The Gallery can host substantial guest numbers while still feeling more private and contained than the largest hall, making it suitable for birthdays, corporate functions, community events and milestone occasions.
