We're Here To Set A New Standard In Local Hospitality

Founded with the audacious belief that high-quality hospitality should not be reserved for particular postcodes, we have set out to redefine the local dining experience by creating an environment where talent thrives, creativity flourishes, and communities are united.

At Bart & Taylor, we are all about bringing industry-leading hospitality to a neighbourhood near you. Through creating meaningful experiences, we bring communities together, whether that’s through innovative food and drink, music events, creative meet-ups or employment and training opportunities. Using our optimistic outlook helps bring people together to create moments that help build stronger local communities.
